About Me!

Hi, I'm Cami! I am from New Orleans, Louisiana pursuing a Bachelor of Art degree at Louisiana State University. I am a passionate graphic designer with a love for bringing ideas to life. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I thrive on turning conceptual ideas into captivating designs that strongly resonate with audiences!
